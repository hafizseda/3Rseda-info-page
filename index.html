Welcome to Digital Services Division

The Digital Services Division of SEDA Malaysia champions the strategic integration of technology into our operations, systems, and services. We aim to deliver efficient, secure, and user-friendly digital experiences aligned with the authority's energy development goals.

  • Enhancing cybersecurity with MFA, WAF, and vulnerability remediation
  • Optimizing HR, network, and document management systems
  • Supporting new building IT deployment (network, telephony, internet)
  • Overseeing procurement, licensing, and staff training support
